About me

Hi! I’m Sonya, a web designer who loves creating clean, user-friendly, and visually pleasing websites. For me, design is not just about how things look — it’s also about how they work and feel. I enjoy figuring out how a page should look so that people want to scroll through it, everything makes sense, and nothing feels out of place I’ve worked on different types of projects — from simple landing pages to more complex websites. What I love most is seeing how an idea turns into something real, and how design starts to “live.” I’m always open to new projects, especially the kind where I can add a bit of my own touch and create something that’s both useful and meaningful.
I’m fluent in both Ukrainian and English, so communication is never a problem
